Tab Pages.

Create an app that contains three TabPages.
On first TabPage, place a CheckedListBox with six items.
On second TabPage, place six Textboxes.
On the last TabPage, place six LinkLabels.
- The users selection on the firs TabPage should specify whic of the six LinkLabels will be displayed.
- Use second tab page to modify the web page that is opened by LinkLabels.
